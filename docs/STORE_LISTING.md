**Description** (4000 char max)
```
Vogel is a comprehensive German learning app designed for Turkish speakers. Prepare for Goethe and TELC exams in real exam format, progress step by step through all CEFR levels from A1 to C1.

WHY VOGEL?
Most German learning apps are designed for English speakers. Vogel is built around Turkish thinking patterns — it teaches you how to bridge der/die/das with Turkish (which has no grammatical gender), and how to map Akkusativ vs Dativ onto Turkish case suffixes you already know.

FULL CEFR CURRICULUM, A1 TO C1
• A1 — Beginner: Greetings, numbers, personal info, basic verbs
• A2 — Elementary: Past tense (Perfekt), daily routines, shopping
• B1 — Intermediate: Subordinate clauses, future plans, work, travel
• B2 — Upper-intermediate: Konjunktiv II, Passiv, environment, technology
• C1 — Advanced: Konjunktiv I, idioms, academic language

GOETHE & TELC EXAM PREPARATION
Question banks in real exam format. Separate practice for Lesen (reading), Hören (listening), Schreiben (writing), and Sprechen (speaking). Content aligned with Goethe-Institut's vocabulary lists. Dedicated TELC A1, A2, B1, B2 prep modules.

SPRECHEN (SPEAKING) MODULE
A1 and A2 speaking practice in telc format. Introduce yourself, ask questions, describe photos, role-play conversations. On-device AI-powered speech recognition — audio never sent to servers.

5000+ WORDS + VERB CONJUGATIONS
Vocabulary aligned with Goethe-Institut standards. Every noun shown with its article (der/die/das). Irregular verb conjugations (Präsens, Präteritum, Perfekt) one tap away. Meaningful example sentences.

SMART SPACED REPETITION
Spaced repetition algorithm shows you words right before you forget them. Adaptive difficulty adjusts the challenge to your pace.

CROSS-DEVICE SYNC
Sign in with Email, Google, or Apple. Pick up on iPad where you left off on iPhone. All progress securely synced via Firestore.

WORKS OFFLINE
Continue lessons without internet. On a bus, on a plane, in a subway tunnel — learn German anywhere.

DAILY HABIT SYSTEM
Streak tracking, XP points, leagues, achievement badges. A few minutes a day reaches B2 in 3-6 months.

PREMIUM MEMBERSHIP
• Unlimited hearts — finish a lesson without interruption
• Unlimited access to all levels (A1-C1)
• Full proficiency exam simulator modules
• Complete Sprechen (speaking) modules
• Cross-device sync
• Family sharing supported

Premium is an auto-renewable subscription. Cancel anytime in your Apple ID settings.
```
